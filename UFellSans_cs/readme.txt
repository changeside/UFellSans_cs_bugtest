--Readme--

Greetings! This ghost of the Underfell version of Sans has options to toggle extremely harmful jokes, but the options are on by default, keep that in mind please.
Pay attention to how you treat him, he'll remember most of it, which will affect his current mood or basic actions towards you.

--------------------------------------------------------------
|| Some of his functions are ||
- randomly talking to you
- telling jokes
- petting (stroke his mouse over special areas)
- setting images as your background
- alarm functions
- emptying your trash bin
- managing a calendar
- checking emails (needs to be configured yourself)
- displaying your system information
- he also has a growing number of minigames that you can find in his menu

|| Hotkeys ||
Press t to prompt him to talk
Press r to make him repeat himself
Press f1 to open the readme file
